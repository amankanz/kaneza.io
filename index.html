My Journey into Tech: From YouTube to Full-Stack Ambition

Introduction

Hello, world! My name is Kaneza, and I'm excited to share my first journal entry with you. This space will be where I document my experiences, challenges, and triumphs as I navigate the path of becoming a full-stack developer. Today,I want to take you through the story of how I found my passion for technology and decided to pursue a career in tech....

The Spark of Curiosity

My journey began with a simple curiosity about how websites worked. I remember spending hours online, fascinated by the seamless interaction between users and digital platforms. One day, I decided to take matters into my own hands. I turned to YouTube, that vast sea of knowledge, and started watching tutorials on HTML and CSS. The thrill of seeing my first lines of code transform into a functional webpage was indescribable. I was hooked.

The Self-Taught Hustle

For months, I immersed myself in the world of web development through YouTube. I followed tutorials, built small projects, and slowly started to understand the fundamentals of coding. But as I delved deeper, I realized that I needed more than just online videos. I needed structure, guidance, and a community of like-minded individuals who shared my passion.

Taking the Leap with Kreativstrom

That's when I decided to enroll in a training program with Kreativstrom. I knew that formal training would not only provide me with a solid foundation in web development but also give me the opportunity to connect with other aspiring tech professionals. The program was intense, covering everything from basic HTML and CSS to more advanced JavaScript. It was challenging, but every bit worth it.

Embracing the Challenge

Balancing my studies with personal responsibilities was no easy feat. There were late nights, early mornings, and countless moments of doubt. But each challenge only strengthened my resolve. I learned to manage my time better, stay focused, and push through obstacles. The sense of accomplishment I felt after completing each project kept me motivated.

The Next Chapter: ZTM Academy

After completing my training with Kreativstrom, I knew I wanted to take my skills to the next level. I enrolled in the ZTM Academy, where I am currently expanding my knowledge to become a full-stack developer. The journey is demanding, but the progress I see in myself keeps me motivated. With each new concept I learn, I feel more confident and prepared for the tech industry.

Vision and Goals

My ultimate goal is not only to succeed in the tech industry but to do so in a way that aligns with my values of financial stability, spiritual growth, moral integrity, and good health. I aspire to create solutions that make a positive impact and to inspire others who are on a similar path.
